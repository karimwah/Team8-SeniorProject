About Steganography

Secret message.

Steganography is the practice of concealing information within other non-secret data. It is often used to hide the existence of the communicated data.The first recorded use of steganography dates back to ancient Greece, where secret messages were hidden on wax tablets. In modern digital steganography, data can be hidden within image files, audio files, or even text documents. Steganography plays a crucial role in digital security and can be used for both benign and malicious purposes.
